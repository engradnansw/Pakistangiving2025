Key Insights

💰 Giving Preference

44.7% of donations go directly to people in need, reflecting preference for informal, personal giving over formal charity channels.

🕌 Religious Motivation

Faith-based giving is deeply embedded in culture, with religious obligation being the top reason for 58% of donors.

🤝 Trust in Charities

Pakistanis show higher trust in charities (10.4/15) compared to global average (9.22/15), especially local charities.

🎯 Top Causes

Poverty relief (39%), religious organizations (38%), and homelessness (30%) are the most supported causes.

📈 Frequency

39% of donors give 6+ times per year, showing consistent giving habits tied to religious and cultural occasions.

🔍 Discovery

19% discover charities through local presence, highlighting importance of community visibility over social media.

Source: World Giving Report 2025 | Survey of 1,049 Pakistani respondents | Pakistan Centre for Philanthropy & Charities Aid Foundation
